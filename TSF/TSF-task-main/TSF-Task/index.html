- COVID-19 ORPHAN RELIEF -

More than 1700 children have lost their parents during COVID-19 pandemic. They need care and protection. The child Care Foundation is there to help them in this situation by providing them food, medication and education.

Together we can provide them a better life. Your donation today can help provide life saving relief. For every 100 rupees you donate, one child will not sleep hungry.



It's not the physical pain that endangers orphans the most

It is the mental pain caused by stress from years and years of being neglected, pushed aside, disregarded, unloved, and made to feel undeserving, and in almost all cases, made to feel like a possession rather than an equal human being.
